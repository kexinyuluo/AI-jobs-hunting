# Structural-reference provenance

The fixture authors inspected exactly the two pinned MIT references listed in
`structural-references.yaml`. Inspection copies lived only under
`tmp/web_artifacts/`; they are not part of this corpus.

Only broad, non-expressive structure informed the synthetic recipes: a compact
single-column chronology, standard section order, repeated employer headers, and
ordinary bullet hierarchy. All Jordan Rivers prose, employers, schools, URLs,
metrics, ordering choices, and declarative recipes were written from scratch.
No upstream template binary, TeX source, identity, resume prose, embedded
metadata, style definition, or formatting implementation is redistributed.

The two upstream MIT license texts were retrieved at the pinned commits and are
bundled verbatim in this directory for attribution. Their inclusion does not
mean that upstream expressive content is embedded in the fixtures.
