Senior Platform Engineer

Nimbus Robotics is a fictional company building autonomous carts for synthetic warehouse simulations. Its fleet platform schedules test routes, distributes software updates, and reports health for invented robot fleets.

What you will do

  • Design Go services and REST APIs for fleet scheduling and regional software delivery.
  • Operate distributed systems on AWS and Kubernetes using Terraform and CI/CD automation.
  • Improve PostgreSQL reliability, observability, service-level objectives, and incident recovery.
  • Partner with application engineers on safe rollouts and reusable platform interfaces.

What we are looking for

  • Five or more years building and operating production backend or platform systems.
  • Experience with Go or Python, Kubernetes, AWS, Terraform, PostgreSQL, and REST APIs.
  • Clear communication about tradeoffs, failure modes, and staged delivery.

Location and compensation

This fictional role is remote within the United States. The invented base salary range is $190,000 to $230,000 per year. H-1B transfer sponsorship is available.
