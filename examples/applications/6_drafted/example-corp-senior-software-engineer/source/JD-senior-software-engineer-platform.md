# Senior Software Engineer, Platform — Example Corp

Location: Remote (US)
Employment type: Full-time

## About Example Corp

Example Corp builds developer-facing infrastructure that helps engineering teams
ship and operate services with confidence. Our platform team owns the internal
APIs, automation, and observability that let hundreds of engineers move fast
without reinventing the plumbing underneath every product.

## What you'll do

- Design, build, and operate cloud-native microservices and distributed systems that power our platform
- Own reliable, well-documented REST APIs and gRPC services used by internal teams and third-party developers
- Build event-driven pipelines and background processing with strong delivery and consistency guarantees
- Improve observability with structured logging, metrics, and distributed tracing, and define service-level objectives
- Automate provisioning and deployment with infrastructure-as-code and modern CI/CD
- Partner with product, support, and SRE teams to improve reliability, performance, and developer experience

## What we're looking for

- 5+ years of experience building backend services in production
- Strong programming skills in one or more of Python, Go, or Java
- Hands-on experience with AWS, Docker, and Kubernetes
- Experience with relational databases such as PostgreSQL and caches such as Redis
- Familiarity with microservices, distributed systems, and event-driven architecture
- Experience with Terraform, CI/CD, and Git-based workflows
- A track record of building observable, well-tested systems and mentoring other engineers

## Nice to have

- Experience designing public APIs and client SDKs
- Experience with high-throughput messaging and queueing systems
- Interest in platform and developer-tooling problems
