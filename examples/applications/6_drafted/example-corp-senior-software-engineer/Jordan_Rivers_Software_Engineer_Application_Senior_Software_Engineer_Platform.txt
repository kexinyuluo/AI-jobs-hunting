PAST EXPERIENCE
===============
At Northwind Systems I have worked as a senior software engineer across the backend and platform stack. I led a payments platform migration into independently deployable microservices, introducing idempotent transaction workflows, contract tests, and canary releases that cut deploy time from hours to minutes and reduced failed-payment incidents by 40%.

I also built an event-driven notifications pipeline handling more than 50 million events per day with at-least-once delivery, backpressure, and dead-letter queues, and shipped a public API gateway exposing REST and gRPC endpoints with shared authentication, rate limiting, and request validation. Alongside that product work, I rolled out structured logging, metrics, and distributed tracing across more than thirty services and established the service-level objectives and on-call runbooks the team adopted as its standard. These projects consistently combined hands-on distributed-systems engineering with a focus on reliability, observability, and developer experience.
